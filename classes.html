Beginner

(Ages 5+)

Discover the joy of movement and rhythm.
Students are introduced to:
• Basic adavus (steps)
• Hand gestures (mudras)
• Postures and expressions (bhavas)
• Rhythm (talom) and classical music

Intermediate

(Ages 10+ or 1–2 years of training)

Refining form, expression, and stage presence.
Students will learn:
• Jathis (short dance sequences)
• Abhinaya (facial expression)
• Performance structure (jatiswarams, sabdams)
• Clarity, stamina, and polish

Advanced

(By audition or Guru recommendation only)

Mastering artistry with discipline.
• Full-length items like varnams, padams
• Brass plate dancing (Tarangam)
• Natya Shastra-based theory
• Stage and annual production readiness
