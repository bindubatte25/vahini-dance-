Our Story

Vahini - The Dance Academy was founded with a vision to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Indian classical dance. Rooted in tradition yet embracing modern pedagogy, the academy serves as a beacon for aspiring dancers who seek to master the art of expression through movement.

With a deep commitment to excellence, Vahini nurtures students in an environment that fosters discipline, creativity, and cultural awareness. Our academy has trained numerous artists who have gone on to perform on prestigious stages, keeping the spirit of Kuchipudi alive for generations to come.

Kuchipudi

Originating from Andhra Pradesh, Kuchipudi is a vibrant dance form known for its graceful movements, quick footwork, and expressive storytelling. It blends rhythmic sequences with narrative elements, making it a captivating art form.

What Students Will Learn: Basic and advanced techniques, storytelling through expressions (Abhinaya), and intricate footwork.
